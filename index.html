About Me

Hello! My name is Aaron Jefferson, and I am currently a second-year student at the University of Dundee, studying BSc Computer Science with Industrial Placement. My skills specialise in Front-end Web development, Android App development, and UI/UX development, though I'm also a product design and marketing enthusiast, and I've also got a keen interest in politics and history.

Ever since I was very young, I have been facinated by computers, and how they and apps work. Right through secondary school, whether it was in Computing class, or in my own free time, I would be building user-friendly apps to solve specific problems, or to teach myself new skills.

For example, in 2021, I taught myself HTML and CSS development by building a hotel booking and restaurant ordering app for a hypothetical hotel/restaurant business, and progressed those skills to build an Android, Windows (both C# WinForms and WinUI), and web app, incorporating Javascript along the way.
Earlier this year, in an attempt to teach myself Dart and Flutter development, I re-wrote, from scratch, the original Android WebView app into a native Flutter application, which could run on Android, Web, MacOS, and Windows, from a single codebase.

I am also in the process of learning Tailwind JS for web development. I also learned Java in first year, and I am now in the process of learning C and C++ in second year at university.

Furthermore, I am also very much into nature and wildlife photography, and vinyl record collection.
